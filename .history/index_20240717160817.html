Short Bio

I start my Ph.D at SMART@HKUST on 2022 Fall, advised by Prof. Chen hao (2022-Now). I also work with Prof. Lin Luo (2024-Now)

I did my master (2018-2021) as well as bachelor (2014-2018) at the Sun Yat-sen University, where I was advised by Ruixuan Wang, Jianguo Zhang, and Wei-Shi Zheng. I have also spent a year at the Tencent Youtu Lab working as a research intern (2020-2021). I was an engineer at Pengcheng Lab working with Prof. Wei Gao and Dr. Tong Zhang on computer vision and machine learning (2021-2022).

I'm interested in computer vision and medical image analysis, especially self-supervision and data imbalance.
