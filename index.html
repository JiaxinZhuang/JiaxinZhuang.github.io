About Me

I am a 2nd year master student at Dept. of Data science and computing, Sun Yat-sen University. Currently, I am advised by Prof. Ruixuan Wang, Wei-shi Zheng and Jianguo Zhang at iSEE-SYSU. I received my B.Sc. Degree from Dept. of Data science and computing, Sun Yat-sen University in 07/2018.
