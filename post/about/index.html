About Me

I am a 2nd year master student at Dept. of Data science and computing, Sun Yat-sen University. Currently, i am advised by Prof. Ruixuan Wang, Wei-shi Zheng and Jianguo Zhang at iSEE-SYSU. I received my B.Sc. Degree from Dept. of Data science and computing, Sun Yat-sen University in 07/2018. I was a research intern of Human Cyber Physical Intelligence Integration Lab, advised by Prof. Liang Lin.

My research interest lie within deep learning for computer vision, particalurly in interpretability, imbalanced data and unsupverised learning, clustering.

News!!: I am seeking for Ph.D from 2021 Fall. Feel free to reach out to me if you are interested.
